WELCOME TO BETTER LIFE THERAPY

About Us

Our therapists know that the human experience is often painful. We aim to be a blend of support, teacher, and witness to your story and growth.

Better Life Therapy offers competent counseling at low to moderate fees to support all members of the community.

We accept a number of major commercial insurance companies.

Imagine what your life could be if you weren’t plagued by symptoms of your anxiety, OCD, depression, or eating disorder. You might be more available to the important people in your life. You might be more free to enjoy the moment. Or maybe you would go for the job you don’t think you couldn’t get.

Each of our therapists believes therapy can have a monumental impact in your life, in the life of your child, and in your relationships.

We are most easily reached via email at betterlifecolorado@gmail.com. You can also call to set up a free phone consultation with one of our clinicians at (720) 445-9979.

“ Healing is possible–even from severe trauma, OCD, anxiety, depression, and eating disorders. We assert this not from unbridled optimism, but from experience with real clients. ”

Our Goal

We focus on doing what works. That means using a combination of approaches based on who you are and what you’re looking to get out of therapy.

Therapy looks a bit different for each person. It may be focused on exploring and making sense of your story. It may include learning concrete skills for changing thought and behavior patterns. It may involve work outside of sessions. We’ll talk about your preferences and together, we’ll develop a plan that will move you toward your goals.

For those interested, our approach is rooted in a combination of several approaches, including cognitive-behavioral therapy, acceptance and commitment therapy, dialectical behavior therapy, internal family systems and mindfulness-based therapy.
