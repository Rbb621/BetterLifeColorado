Stef Brannan

Pre-Licensed Social Worker
she/her/hers

I believe that one of the bravest things you can do is to be vulnerable.

Taking steps to seek therapy is intimidating. I provide a non-judgmental atmosphere that is client-centered, empathetic, and empowering. You are the expert on your life. I am honored to walk alongside you on a path of wellness. You were put on this earth to thrive. It’s time to make that happen. Let’s breathe together (well, figuratively) and uncover how to help you achieve all of your goals.

Life is complicated. Mental health is a vital piece of our being. Free yourself from the grips of depression, anxiety, trauma, and relational conflicts to find a renewed purpose and meaning. Your relationships will be positively impacted, and you may just find a new level of self-love.

I enjoy working with adults and adolescents. Therapy sessions are tailored to each client’s personal values, needs, and goals. Together we will identify key target areas to center our focus. I help my clients not only obtain peace of mind, but also peace of heart and peace of soul. Let’s go find hope together!

Approaches: Acceptance and Commitment therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Motivational Interviewing
