Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

Better Life Therapy offers compassionate outpatient treatment for individuals with OCD.

Subtypes of obsessive compulsive disorder we treat

Contamination obsessions

Hoarding

Harm obsessions

Sin, religion, morality or scrupulosity OCD

Aggressive obsessions

Sexual obsessions

Relationship OCD

Specialists in OCD Treament

Better Life Therapy has therapists who specialize in the treatment of OCD. Our compassionate therapists will assess the severity of your obsessive compulsive disorder and help you overcome obsessive thinking and compulsive behaviors. Contact us today to set up your first appointment.

Our therapists use the gold-standard treatment for Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder known as Exposure and Response (or Ritual) Prevention.
