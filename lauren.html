Lauren Davis

Licensed Professional Counselor Candidate

You are worthy of healing and happiness, and you already hold all the strength within yourself to create the life you want. We are not meant to walk this journey alone, and I would be honored to guide you in finding your purpose, develop self-acceptance, and learn the skills needed to cope with the many stressors in life that we face. Therapy is not a one-size-fits-all experience, and you deserve a counselor who takes the time to get to know your unique needs and challenges. I believe that throughout life, people learn unhealthy behaviors as a means of survival, and unfortunately, we can become stuck in those negative patterns. I can help you to unlearn those habits and commit to a healthier and happier way of living. My goal is to make space for you to have a voice, and my hope is that your time in therapy is something you can truly enjoy and benefit from.

As a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor, I have worked with a variety of mental and physical disabilities ranging from autism spectrum disorder to generalized anxiety disorder, OCD, and PTSD. No matter what challenges you are facing, a diagnosis does not have to be a life-sentence of unhappiness. I use a Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy Approach to help individuals challenge their negative thoughts and develop healthy coping skills. I love to blend traditional therapeutic techniques with skills needed to develop strong mind-body connection, such as EFT tapping, deep breathing exercises, and living an overall healthy lifestyle. Together, we will develop the goals that are most important to you and create a roadmap of how to reach those goals. Whether you are struggling to overcome obsessive thoughts, love yourself more authentically, or want to discover a more fulfilling career field, I will gently push and support you to get to where you want to be. No goal is too small or too grand to be within reach.

I stand in solidarity with BIPOC, the LGBTQ community, neurodivergent individuals, and people with varying abilities. When you are with me, you are free to talk about anything without fear of being judged. Reaching out for help is the hardest step, so if you have gotten this far you should feel so proud. Now take a deep breath, you got this!
